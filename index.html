Words in a book!

Welcome to Words in a book. To get started, upload a .txt file below.

When you are done, hit list counts to see all words by their count, and hit list primes to see all numbers that are prime numbers.

When you're done, open up the console. I recommend using Google Chrome's console.

To get the occurences of each word in this book, enter:

processor.getWords(fullText);

This will show you a list of all words ordered by occurence in descending order.
